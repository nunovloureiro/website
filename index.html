Nuno Loureiro. Porto, 1992.


Sound and Image degree at Escola das Artes da Universidade Católica Portuguesa. 2013.

Master in Multimedia at Faculdade de Engenharia da Universidade do Porto. Interactive Music and Sound Design specialization. 2018.


Solo music as lorr no and mada treku. Member of fugly, solar corona, úz, milteto and ogbe. Richie Culver collaborator.


Currently programming and setting up the quadriphonic series of concerts 'rasgo', in porto and braga, with Inês Malheiro.


Collaborated with André Coelho, António Serginho, Atillla, João Sarnadas, Jonathan Uliel Saldanha, Jorge Queijo, José Alberto Gomes, Inês Malheiro, Luís Kasprzykowski, Miguel Ramos, Peixe and Polido, among others, in spaces such as Fundação de Serralves, Casa da Música, Galeria Zé dos Bois, Maus Hábitos, and festivals like Supernova, Paredes de Coura, Super Bock Super Rock and Milhões de Festa.


Frequency ranges representing slow earth frequencies, audible frequencies and visible frequencies inspired by the Spectrum of Waves, as shown in Intermediary Spaces, book by Eliane Radigue and Julia Eckhardt.


Website developed by myself.

EVERYTHING IS BEAUTIFUL AND NOTHING HURTS

EIBANH is a DIY online radio that shuffles 10 albums a month to share local music. Video synthesis using Hydra. Radio transmission using Icecast.

RASGO

Quadriphonic series of events around the year occuring at Arte Total, in Braga, and Hotelier, in Porto. Past artists include Gonçalo Penas, Vasco Lé, Wordclock and Well.

RAMAL

Independent music platform operating in Porto releasing showcases by local artists. Curated by me and André Coelho.

FAVELA DISCOS

Favela Discos is a label and art collective founded in Porto, 2013. Focusing mostly on experimental music.

Complete discography here!

KAPUA WAIATA

Multimedia installation for families. Previously on Festival MI, Correntes de um só rio and Festival Papagaio.

R+R+R=RÁDIO

A fun workshop for kids where they think about what radio is, write and record their own segments.

SOUND SYNTHESIS AND NON-LINEAR COMPOSITION

Introductory workshop on different sound synthesis techniques and non-linear compositional approaches using Pure Data at Faculdade de Belas Artes do Porto.

SOLAR CORONA

Solar Corona were born within the creative bubble of a city that shows how to write rock with crooked lines, Barcelos. How it should be. 2022 will see a new full lenght album, now dropping the saxophone and featuring Nuno Loureiro on synths/dub/fx. New album out soon!

MILTETO

The group’s formation and nature varies from concert to concert, both in number of musicians, type of instruments used, in the occupation of space or the improvisation techniques they use. Over the years the project served as a space of experimentation and collaboration between the collective and other musicians from Porto’s scene.

In Trux We Pux 3 out on Favela Discos.
